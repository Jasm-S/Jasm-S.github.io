Virginia Mori

About the artist

Virginia Mori is an Itlaian artist. She's a child of the eighties, born in 1981. Her chosen mediums include pencil, ink, and ballpoint pen on paper. She currently lives and works in Pesaro and Milan, Italy. In addition to tradiotional hand-drawn work, Mori's art can also be found in illustrations and short animations.
